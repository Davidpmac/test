You and your family are warmly welcomed to Ancrum Farm Village

Situated just outside the picturesque village of Ancrum and a scenic ten-minute drive from the royal burgh town of Jedburgh, Ancrum Farm Village offers a wholesome day out for families and nature lovers alike; stocking local produce for anyone wishing to support local traders. Ancrum Farm Village could be your next perfect outing.

  • Meet our friendly farm animals in the petting zoo
  • Explore scenic walking trails
  • Let the kids have fun in our outdoor play park
  • Relax in our farm café with homemade cakes
  • Shop for fresh local produce and plants

Why Visit Us?

Whether you're planning a family day out or just passing by, Acrum Farm Village is a relaxing stop where everyone is welcome. Enjoy nature, good food, and our warm hospitality.
